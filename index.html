Mission Statement

Synthea is a synthetic patient generator that models the medical history of synthetic patients. Our mission is to output high-quality synthetic, realistic but not real, patient data and associated health records covering every aspect of healthcare. The resulting data is free from cost, privacy, and security restrictions. It can be used without restriction for a variety of secondary uses in academia, research, industry, and government (although a citation would be appreciated).

Sample Data

Synthea outputs data in standard formats: HL7 C-CDA and FHIR®, as well as CSV and plain text.

Browse sample data at SyntheticMass or access directly via the FHIR STU3 RESTful API at https://syntheticmass.mitre.org/fhir.

Synthetic data can also be generated as a service for your own public FHIR server.

Download Data and Concepts

1 million synthetic patients can be downloaded from SyntheticMass. A .csv file listing all coded concepts represented in Synthea is also available.
